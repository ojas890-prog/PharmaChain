Frequently Asked Operations

What roles are supported within the verification system architecture?

PharmaChain strictly enforces role-based access controls (RBAC) across four operational entities: System Admins, Manufacturers (write ledger items), Distributors (transit logistics tracking), and Pharmacies (final point-of-sale delivery confirmation).

How does item reservation and stock safety margins interact?

When an order is submitted by a downstream distributor or pharmacy node, those target units are immediately calculated inside a reserved state pool. This locks items out of double-purchasing configurations while transit rules execute.
